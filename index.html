The Process

1. First Meeting

At first, we find a client and discuss what they expect from the website we’ll create, how much time it will take us and who will host it.

2. Design

Design

3. Development

Our team cooperatively starts building a website based on our plan and the first discussion. We also apply SEO best practices to expose the website to a maximum number of internet users.

4. Testing

We apply various tests and examine the website on all regular devices and browsers to ensure that our responsive design is working flawlessly.

5. Second Meeting

After finishing our testing and removing any bugs, we show our client the website and ask them to point out any issues they might have with it. Then we will edit our website with our client’s concerns in mind.

6. Release

At last, our client’s final product will be published to the web. If requested, we will also take care of the hosting of their website.
